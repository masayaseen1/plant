About us

why follows us?

Plants are the silent superheroes of our planet. They not only provide us with delicious food and clean air, but also create habitats for animals and help regulate the climate. Taking care of plants, by watering them properly and giving them enough light, isn't just about keeping them pretty. It's about ensuring a healthy environment for all living things, ourselves included. So we, a group of developers and farmers, decided to create a website to teach people how to care for their plants
